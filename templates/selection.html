Analog Clock

An analog clock uses rotating hands to show time:

  • Short hand: Hours (1-12)
  • Long hand: Minutes (0-59)
  • Thin hand: Seconds (0-59)

Reading Minutes

Each number on the clock represents 5 minutes when reading the minute hand. For example, when the minute hand points to 3, it means 15 minutes.
